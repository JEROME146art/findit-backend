Ranked-Choice Elimination Simulator

Why does our college use Ranked-Choice Voting? In traditional elections with 4 candidates, someone can win with only 26% of the vote despite 74% of the campus preferring someone else! With Ranked-Choice, if no candidate achieves >50% majority in Round 1, the candidate with the fewest votes is eliminated and their voters' 2nd choices are transferred automatically until a true majority winner emerges.
